1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Jobble ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our job application tracking service at https://jobble.app ("Service").

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you create an account and use our Service, we collect information you provide, including:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, signup reason, preferences
  • Job Search Data: Applications, job titles, descriptions, salary information, application status
  • Company Information: Company names, websites, descriptions, locations, sizes
  • Contact Information: Professional contacts, names, emails, phone numbers, LinkedIn profiles
  • Interview Data: Interview types, formats, dates, feedback, outcomes
  • Notes and Documentation: Personal notes, interview preparation, feedback
  • Offer Information: Salary details, benefits, terms, negotiation notes
  • Resume Files: Uploaded resume documents and related information

2.2 Information We Collect Automatically

When you use our Service, we automatically collect:

  • Usage Data: Pages viewed, features used, time spent, click patterns
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers
  • Activity Logs: Login times, actions taken, platform used (web, mobile)
  • Performance Data: Loading times, errors, technical diagnostics

2.3 Information from Third Parties

We may receive information about you from:

  • Authentication Services: Clerk (our authentication provider)
  • Analytics Services: Usage and performance analytics
  • Public Sources: Publicly available company or professional information

5. Your Privacy Rights and Choices

5.1 Opt Out of Data Sales

You have the right to opt out of the sale of your personal information.

How to Opt Out: Log into your Jobble account and navigate to your user profile page. Look for the data privacy settings where you can disable data sales.

Effect of Opting Out: We will stop selling your personal information to third parties for commercial purposes. However, we retain the right to use your data for internal business operations and may continue to share anonymized or aggregated data.

5.2 Account Management

  • Update Information: You can update your profile information through your account settings
  • Delete Account: You can delete your account through the app or by emailing us
  • Data Export: Contact us to request a copy of your data

5.3 Communication Preferences

  • You can manage email preferences through your account settings
  • You cannot opt out of essential service communications
