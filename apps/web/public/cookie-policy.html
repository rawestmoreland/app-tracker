Cookie Policy

Last updated: October 2, 2025

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience by remembering your preferences and understanding how you use our site.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to analyze how visitors use our website and to improve our services. Specifically, we use Google Analytics to collect information about your visit.

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable basic features like remembering your cookie consent choice.

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
cookie-consent-apptrack Remembers your cookie consent preference 1 year

Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously. We use Google Analytics for this purpose.

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
_ga Used to distinguish users and sessions 2 years
_gid Used to distinguish users 24 hours
_gat Used to throttle request rate 1 minute

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to help us understand how our visitors use the website. Google Analytics collects information such as:

  • How often users visit our site
  • What pages they visit
  • What other sites they used prior to coming to our site
  • Browser and device information

Google Analytics does not collect personally identifiable information. The data collected is used solely to improve our website and user experience.

For more information about how Google uses data, please visit: How Google uses data when you use our partners' sites or apps

Your Choices

When you first visit our website, you'll see a cookie banner asking for your consent to use analytics cookies. You can choose to:

  • Accept: Allow us to use Google Analytics cookies
  • Decline: Use the website without analytics cookies

You can change your cookie preferences at any time by clicking the "Cookie Settings" link in our website footer.

Browser Controls

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can set your browser to refuse cookies or delete certain cookies. However, please note that if you block or delete cookies, some features of our website may not work properly.
